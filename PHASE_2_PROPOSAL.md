## 2. GOALS & OUTCOMES

## 3. WHAT YOU GET

### A) Financial & Statistics Tracking

Your users can manage their poker journey:

- **Bankroll Balance** with transaction history and trend visualization
- **Session Tracking** for both cash games and tournaments
- **Session History** with filters, search, and "Import from Another App"
- **Statistics Dashboard** showing Total Profit, ROI, ITM Ratio, Hourly Rate
- **Casino Balances** to track funds across multiple sites

*Bankroll Screen*
> Track your balance, view transaction history, and see profit trends over time.

*Statistics Dashboard*
> Comprehensive stats including ROI, ITM ratio, hourly rate, and win percentages.

*Session History*
> Log and review all your poker sessions with detailed filtering options.

---

### B) Real-Time & Social Features

Keep your community engaged:

- **Live Streaming** with YouTube, Twitch, and Facebook embed support
- **Basic Global Chat** for real-time community interaction
- **Social Feed** with posts, likes, comments, and player following
- **Real-Time Standings** with live chip count updates during events

*Live Now Section*
> Watch live poker streams directly in the app with LIVE badges on tournament cards.

*Global Chat*
> Community chat room where users can discuss events, hands, and connect.

*Social Feed*
> Follow players, share posts, and engage with the poker community.

---

### C) Poker Utility Tools

Help your users improve their game:

- **Deal/ICM Calculator** for final table negotiations
- **Hand Ranges** builder with visual 13x13 matrix
- **Equity vs Hand** calculator with Monte Carlo simulation
- **Notepad** for quick notes and session thoughts

*ICM Calculator*
> Calculate deal equity based on chip stacks and payout structures.

*Hand Ranges*
> Create, save, and share custom hand ranges by position.

*Equity Calculator*
> Calculate your winning odds against opponent ranges.

---

### D) Advanced Player Features

For the serious poker community:

- **Player Profiles** with tournament history and statistics
- **Leaderboard Rankings** across different metrics
- **Player Notes** to track opponents and playing styles
- **Alerts & Notifications** for events, results, and social activity

*Player Profile*
> View player stats, tournament results, and follow their journey.

*Leaderboard*
> See top players ranked by earnings, ROI, and other metrics.

---

### E) Enterprise & Reporting

Professional-grade features:

- **Staking on Pokerbase** - Buy and sell tournament action
- **Hand Replays** - Log and review hand histories
- **CSV Export** - Export session data for analysis
- **PDF Export** - Generate reports for taxes and sponsors

*Staking Marketplace*
> Browse available stakes, sell your own action, track your investments.

---

### F) Enhanced Admin Dashboard

Your team gains powerful new tools:

- **Stream Management** - Add/manage live stream embeds
- **Chat Moderation** - Delete messages, monitor activity
- **Player Management** - Enhanced profiles and standings
- **Statistics Overview** - Platform engagement metrics
- **Export Tools** - Generate reports and data exports

---

**Important Note:** Phase 2 focuses on player tools, social features, and live streaming. It does **not** include real-money transactions for buy-ins, gambling features, or payment processing for stakes (staking is information/tracking only).
